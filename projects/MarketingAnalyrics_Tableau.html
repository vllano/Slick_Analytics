Marketing Analytics Portfolio

TThis strategic dashboard furnishes a holistic and granular perspective on key marketing performance indicators (KPIs) and user engagement metrics. By centralizing these critical data points, the dashboard empowers marketing stakeholders to conduct rigorous campaign efficacy analysis, gain actionable insights into user journeys across digital touchpoints, and facilitate data-driven decision-making for optimal strategic adjustments and resource allocation. This dataset was synthesized and curated in Python, ensuring data integrity and analytical flexibility.

Featured Dashboard: UTM Performance

This dashboard analyzes the performance of marketing campaigns tracked by UTM parameters. It provides insights into key metrics such as website traffic, user engagement, and conversion rates across different marketing channels and campaigns. Use the filters and interactive elements within the dashboard to explore the data and gain a deeper understanding of campaign effectiveness.

  • Key Metrics: Traffic, Sessions, Bounce Rate, Click-through rate, Conversion Rate, and Revenue
  • Dimensions: Source, Medium, Campaign, Content, and Date
  • Interactivity: Filters for date ranges, campaigns, and traffic sources.
  • Visualization: Interactive charts and tables for trend analysis and performance comparison.
  • Benefits: Improved decision-making, Increased efficiency, Enhanced accountability, and Better understanding of customers.
